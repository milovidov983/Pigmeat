Reporting a Vulnerability

Do not publicly disclose a vulnerability for at least 30 days.

To report a vulnerability, email em@emsa.cf, and expect a response within 30 days. If the matter cannot be privately resolved, make a GitHub issue and collaborate to resolve it.
